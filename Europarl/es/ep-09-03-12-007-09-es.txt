Informe 2008 sobre los progresos realizados por Croacia (votación) Antes de la votación sobre la enmienda 13: Gracias, señor Presidente.
La enmienda dice así en inglés: "Recalls the informal agreement reached on 26 August 2007 by the Prime Ministers of Croatia and Slovenia on the submission of their border dispute to an international body; welcomes the readiness of Croatia and Slovenia to accept the mediation offer made by the Commission and takes the view that this mediation should be based on international law; in this context, looks forward to a rapid advancement of the accession negotiations;".
