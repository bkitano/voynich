Ship-source pollution and penalties for infringements (debate) The next item is the report by Luis de Grandes Pascual, on behalf of the Committee on Transport and Tourism, on the proposal for a directive of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Directive 2005/35/EC on ship-source pollution and on the introduction of penalties for infringements - C6-0142/2008 -.
The vote will take place tomorrow (Tuesday, 5 May 2009).
