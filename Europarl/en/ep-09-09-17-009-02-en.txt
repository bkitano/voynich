Kazakhstan: the case of Yevgeny Zhovtis I have received five motions for a resolution concerning Kazakhstan: the Yevgeny Zhovtis case (Rule 122).
Kazakh laws which are based on international law must be applied appropriately and transparently in legal proceedings against the human rights activists Yevgeny Zhovtis, Yesingepov and Dubanov.
The Commission fully supports the EU Presidency statement on this case at the OSCE Permanent Council of 10 September.
